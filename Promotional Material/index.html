Our Focus

Our project is focusing on the knowledge sharing. Elderly people are our main target because some of the elderly people are experienced on different aspects, such as cooking, gardening, fishing. Also, elderly people mostly have plenty of time. Therefore, our project would help elderly people enriching their life as well as sharing their knowledge.

MEET OUR PEOPLE

The Issues

Most of elderly feel bored, because they do not have circles of friends. In other words, they do not have any chances to make new friends in order to enrich their life. According to our questionnaire, 90% of the elderly people want to have more lifestyle. However, almost 100% of them have no ideas about how to make new friends or join any events.
Breaking the social barrier
A large amount of elderly people are socially isolated for they feel difficult to contact with their relatives, friends and medical first aid. Thus they require a platform that helps them develop hobbies, maintain and enhance their social networks. Because social network has failed to connect them in a meaningful way, the problem with most social networks for elder people is: the new technology is a big challenge for them, and it is difficult to meet people with shared interests online and have real conversations with them.
A new concept addressing the issue
It is a good problem space for us to make a change for them, elder people have a lot of advantage compare to other people: They are more experienced in specific area, they also have lots of free time, but we tend to overlook and we are wasting their talent and experience, because we don't know how to use it in the right place. By utilising information technology, we can build an online interactive platform that helps them develop hobbies, maintain and enhance their social networks to make the most of knowledge and experience from elderly people.
Make things happen
By using elder people’s potential to share their experience and skill to other people, we can help them maintaining regular contact with the outside world in order to stay happy and healthy. We also need to think about cognitive challenges, means of supporting naturalistic social interaction and emotional needs.

Generates a community.

A large amount of elderly people are socially isolated for they feel difficult to contact with their relatives, friends and medical first aid. Thus they require a platform that helps them develop hobbies, maintain and enhance their social networks.

A new kind of conception.

Our concept would be creating a web based application for elderly where user able to post and share their knowledge or experience regarding their interest and passion for others to view and learn. It has similar feature like blog post where they can post text, images or video online, but it will be less complex where elderly able to understand and make use of the feature easily. All the post will be categorised by tag, for example if they post some cooking recipe the tag will be under “cooking” section. Each post has a comment section for user to comment.

Single Goal.

Pushing people towards a common goal lets people engage with each other in an open and freely accessible environment. People can share experiences they’ve had with each other and create bonds that can last a lifetime. The single goal is easy to achieve, but drives people to communicate and socialise to achieve it, helping to break down the issues of social exclusion.

Our Features

The purpose of our website is helping elderly people to find other elderly with same interests in order to enrich their lifestyle. In our website, users are able to browse other blogpost that they are interested after register. Users can write comments under every blogpost in order to have more communication and interaction.

Steps to start:

01 Register an account
During the signup process, user can select their interest in the selection fields, they have a few default selection such as gardening, cooking, tailoring, and much more for user to choose. User also able to select multiple interests based on their preference.
02 Filling in the interests that you would like to share with others
After successful login they will be navigated to the main page where it is consist of multiple post shared by other users. All the post were filtered and categorised according to user’s field of interest.
03 Find blog post that you are interested in
Users are also able to browse and search for blog posts based on their interest. The information will be shown clearly and logically, such as listed by popularity or page view. And the web-based application also has an attractive social component.
04 Invite people that have the same interest with you
Our website has function of social technology like online chat room, where elderly can find and connect to other people that share their hobby or interest by joining or creating the social group with similar hobby and interest, so they can chat with each other and release their status.
05 Organise an event
Users can organise an outdoor social event and invite other people to join, at the same time they also can join other events created by other people.

Design Process

Original research was completed through a series of interviews, a survey and some observational studies. This research then informed our concept creation. We completed a number of design exercises including bodystorming and concept mapping in order to refine our original idea.
1.Concept Website

In our concept website, we named our website as “ A slice of Life” which means users can share their life every second. In our homepage, we would like to put the most favourite blog posts, which is more attractive to the users. As long as user is interested in one of these blog posts, they could click in and view more specific information about that blog post.

2. Chatroom

The feature of our website is the chatroom. It’s not very common that chatroom is included in a blog website. In our website, we would like to achieve a multi-users chatroom, because it is amazing that when users are able to invite others with same interests into a same chatroom. It is also convience for those users.

3. Proto.io.

We used Proto Io to achieve our prototype, because Proto.Io can help us doing a beautiful and attractive prototype. Also, Proto.io is able to bring users a real website feeling. However, proto.Io is not able to help our chatroom function. Therefore, we tried to find another software which is able to achieve that.

4. Final website prototype

We successfully build our website prototype with working functionality such as navigation menu, buttons, publish post, comment, login and register. We connected our website to a domain hosted by Wix, while refining and adjusting the content layout of the website.
